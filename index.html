How it works

Calculating the top five

The watchlist groups vulnerabilities from CISA’s Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog by CWE, then prioritizes weaknesses linked to more exploited vulnerabilities, recent activity, and ransomware. FIRST EPSS data adds context about the likelihood of future exploitation. The resulting score is a project-specific guide, not an official risk rating.
